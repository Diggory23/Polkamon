Ajolomon

Ajolomon is a sturdy, pinkish mythical creature of Chichen Itza, Mexico. This specie is incredibly strange and rare and worshipped by Mayans. You obtain one by joining an Axolotl and a Python. The story goes as follows, one day the mayans needed a savior from the terrible web spiders of the area. These monstruosus beings terrorized the entire village and caused great ilness to it's people. The chief of the village alongside its elder's decided to go the shrine up in the mountain, where they reached and prayed to the gods, demanding a defendor. The gods council saw the despair of the chief and villagers and send Ajolomon: the protector of Chichen Itza, it temple and it's people.
